AI is driving a fundamental shift in how work gets done and how applications are built. As the 2026 Microsoft Work Trend Index Report highlights, a growing share of workers are moving beyond asking questions to handing off entire tasks and orchestrating multi-agent systems. The challenge is no longer model capability, but consistent, shared data context across the business.

Developers and business users know what they want to automate, and today’s models can deliver. Every new agent starts from zero, relearning how the business works, where data lives, and what rules to follow. Without a consistent foundation, agents can’t coordinate or scale.

That’s the challenge we are solving with Microsoft Fabric .
