Why this matters

At the heart of Microsoft Discovery is the Discovery Engine that mimics the scientific method where specialized agents reason over large amounts of knowledge, generate hypotheses, and validate them in a complex tree across a vast search space. It reframes risk for teams that automate sensitive workflows and rely on third-party data flows.

Operational takeaways

  • This next phase reflects what we’ve learned as we continue to broaden access to enterprise-grade, agentic AI capabilities for R&D.
  • Microsoft Discovery is an extensible platform that brings together agentic orchestration, advanced reasoning, a graph-based knowledge foundation, and high-performance computing.
