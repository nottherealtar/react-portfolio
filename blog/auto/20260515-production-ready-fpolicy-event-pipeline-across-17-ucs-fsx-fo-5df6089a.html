Why this matters

As the number of UCs grows, routing should be treated as configuration data and used to generate both EventBridge rules and routing tests to prevent drift. It reframes risk for teams that automate sensitive workflows and rely on third-party data flows. It highlights where brittle integrations become operational debt as vendors iterate quickly.
