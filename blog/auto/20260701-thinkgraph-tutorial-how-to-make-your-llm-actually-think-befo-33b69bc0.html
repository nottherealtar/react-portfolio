ThinkGraph Tutorial: How to Make Your LLM Actually Think Before Answering

In my previous article , I introduced ThinkGraph -- an open-source pipeline that forces LLMs to decompose complex prompts into atomic fact DAGs before answering. The benchmark scores triage accuracy at 80%, compression quality at 70%, and vote consistency at 64% -- solid baselines that improve with use. Honest answer: don't use it for simple Q&A ("What is the capital of France?"). The triage stage catches trivial prompts and short-circuits.
